A good element of guidance is practicable

note

Summary: A rule or advice tells people in a practical situation how to act. More SBVR principles for good elements of guidance

A good element of guidance is practicable. It must express something that can be applied in practical situations without further interpretation: it needs to be more definite than a general guideline. SBVR's definition of element of guidanceis practicable is :

”The element of guidance is sufficiently detailed and precise that a person who knows the element of guidance can apply it effectively and consistently in relevant circumstances to know what behaviour is acceptable or not [...]” (SBVR 1.5)
